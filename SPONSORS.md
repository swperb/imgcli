# Sponsoring imgcli

imgcli is free, MIT-licensed, and dependency-free. If it saves you time, sponsoring
helps fund maintenance, new formats, and the zero-dependency hardening work.

## Sponsor

- **GitHub Sponsors:** https://github.com/sponsors/swperb &nbsp;<sub>(active once enrolled)</sub>
- More platforms are added over time — the live list is the **Sponsor** button on the
  repo, driven by [`.github/FUNDING.yml`](.github/FUNDING.yml).

## What your sponsorship funds

- **New formats** — TIFF and QOI (zero-dep), plus opt-in WebP/AVIF (see the issues).
- **Maintenance** — security updates, the fuzz/ASan CI, dependency hygiene.
- **Reach** — cross-platform builds and packaging (Windows, more package managers).

## Adding a sponsor platform (maintainer note — the "framework")

The Sponsor button is fully driven by [`.github/FUNDING.yml`](.github/FUNDING.yml).
To connect a new service:

1. Create the account (Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee, Patreon, Open Collective, Liberapay, Polar, …).
2. Uncomment the matching line in `FUNDING.yml` and fill in your username — or add a
   `custom: ["https://..."]` URL for anything not natively supported.
3. Commit. The button updates automatically; no other wiring needed.

Supported keys: `github`, `patreon`, `open_collective`, `ko_fi`, `tidelift`,
`community_bridge`, `liberapay`, `issuehunt`, `lfx_crowdfunding`, `polar`,
`buy_me_a_coffee`, `thanks_dev`, `custom`.
